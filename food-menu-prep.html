Breakfast Entrees

Breakfast Sandwich

Bagel or Biscuit ▼
  1. Cut bagel or biscuit in half.
    • If bagel: toast in toaster 1x before assembling.
  2. Add 1 egg patty.
  3. Add 1 slice American cheese.
  4. Add protein of choice.
    • Ham: 2 slices
    • Turkey sausage: 1 patty
  5. Place on baking sheet (bagel closed) and cook in convection oven.
    • Bagel: 45–60 seconds
    • Biscuit: ~45 seconds
  6. Remove from oven and separate the two halves. Spread 1 oz avocado mash on each side and garnish with Everything Bagel Seasoning, then close the sandwich.
  7. Serve wrapped in brown wrapper.

Avocado Toast

Toast ▼
  1. Toast 1 slice bread — 2 rounds in toaster.
  2. Spread 2 oz avocado spread (~½" thickness) evenly across the bread.
  3. Add a handful (~2 oz) of arugula.
  4. Layer 2 slices of roasted tomatoes.
  5. Sprinkle with Everything Bagel Seasoning.
  6. Cut toast in half diagonally.

Strawberry Banana Toast

Toast ▼
  1. Toast 1 slice bread — 2 rounds in toaster.
  2. Spread 2 oz whipped cream cheese evenly across the bread.
  3. Alternate rows of sliced banana and sliced strawberries horizontally across the toast.
  4. Drizzle with honey.
  5. Tear apart mint leaves and use as garnish.
  6. Cut toast in half.

Lunch Sandwiches

All lunch sandwiches are served with chips and a pickle spear.

Toasted Ham and Cheese

Sourdough ▼
  1. Toast 2 slices sourdough bread — 2x in toaster.
  2. Spread 1 oz balsamic mayo on each slice (2 oz total).
  3. Add a pinch of salt and coarse ground pepper to both slices.
  4. Add 2 oz parmesan on one slice.
  5. Add 2 oz mozzarella on the other slice.
  6. Layer 2 slices of ham on each side (4 slices total).
  7. Add 2 slices of roasted tomato.
  8. Carefully press both sides together.
  9. Toast for 1 minute at 475°F.
  10. Wrap in brown parchment paper and cut in half diagonally.

Turkey Avocado Sandwich

Sourdough ▼

Salads & Wraps

Caesar Salad

Salad ▼
  1. Place 6 oz diced chicken on baking tray and warm in oven for 1 minute.
  2. Add 6 oz romaine lettuce to round to-go bowl (fill to ~90%).
  3. Add ⅓ cup grated parmesan.
  4. Add 6 croutons.
  5. Garnish top with ¼ cup shaved parm.
  6. Add 4 more croutons.
  7. Top with the heated diced chicken.
  8. Add a pinch of cracked black pepper.
  9. Serve 2 oz Caesar dressing on the side in a to-go container.
ServeRound to-go bowl, dressing on the side

Strawberry Walnut Goat Cheese Salad

Salad ▼
  1. Place 6 oz diced chicken on baking tray and warm in oven for 1 minute.
  2. Add 6 oz romaine lettuce to round to-go bowl (fill to ~90%).
  3. Add ⅛ cup goat cheese crumble.
  4. Add ⅓ cup crushed walnuts.
  5. Add ¼ cup diced strawberries.
  6. Top with the heated diced chicken.
  7. Serve 2 oz balsamic vinaigrette on the side in a to-go container.

Spinach Tortilla ▼

  2. Spread 2 oz mayo mix in a thin layer.

Food Prep Recipes

Balsamic Vinaigrette

Dressing ▼
Ingredients
  • 2 cups balsamic vinegar
  • ½ cup honey
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • ⅛ cup ground pepper
  • 1 cup stone ground mustard
  • 1 cup olive oil

Directions

  1. Place balsamic vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper in blender. Blend on setting 1.
  2. Once blended, add 1 cup olive oil. Blend on setting 1 for 5 seconds. Pause. Blend again for 5 more seconds.
  3. Transfer to a plastic container. Label with date made.

Used for: Strawberry Walnut Goat Cheese Salad

Avocado Mash

Spread ▼
Ingredients
  • 15 avocados (pitted)
  • 4–6 tsp salt
  • 4–6 tsp pepper
  • 6–8 tsp Tajín
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ¼–½ cup lime juice

Directions

  1. Place pitted avocado in a large metal bowl.
  2. Add salt, pepper, Tajín, garlic powder, and lime juice.
  3. Use a large masher to rough-mash the mixture.
  4. Finish mixing with a large spatula. Season to taste.
  5. Transfer to a plastic container. Label with date made.

Used for: Avocado Toast and Turkey Avocado Sandwich

Balsamic Spicy Mayo

Sauce ▼
Ingredients
  • 2 cups balsamic vinegar
  • 4 cups spicy mayo
  • 2 cups regular mayo

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend on setting 1 until fully combined.
  2. Once blended, transfer to a large squirt bottle.

Whipped Cream Cheese Spread

Spread ▼
Ingredients
  • 1 stick cream cheese
  • ½ cup whipping cream

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, use a handheld mixer to beat cream cheese until smooth.
  2. Transfer to plastic tubs. Label with item name, date made, and initials.

Brown Sugar Roasted Tomatoes

Prep ▼
Ingredients
  • 30 tomatoes, sliced ½" thick
  • 1 cup + 3 tbsp brown sugar (+ 3 tbsp for top)
  • 1 cup oil
  • 1½ cup balsamic vinegar mixture
  • 4 tbsp salt
  • 3 tbsp pepper
  • 3 tbsp Italian seasoning

Directions

  1. Slice tomatoes into ½" slices.
  2. Marinate overnight in 1 cup brown sugar, salt, 1 cup oil, and ½ cup balsamic vinegar mixture.
  3. When ready to cook, lay marinated slices on a foil-lined baking tray.
  4. Cook in oven for 2–3 minutes.

Whipped Cream

Topping ▼
  1. Add 8 pumps vanilla syrup to the large black whipped cream canister.
  2. Pour heavy whipping cream and fill to the fill line.
  3. Screw the top back on and give it a good shake.
  4. Place a cartridge (found in the cabinet under the drip coffee urns) in the designated spot and twist until you hear the gas escape into the canister.

Additional Prep Guidelines

Romaine Lettuce

Wash and dry the lettuce before cutting. Remove and discard any damaged or unappealing leaves. Cut leaves down the center vein first, then into 1" × 1" sections.

Tomatoes

Wash and clean prior to slicing. Slice to ¼" thickness and place directly in marinating liquid.

Strawberries

Wash and dry before slicing. Discard any damaged or unappealing strawberries. Slice to ⅛" thickness lengthwise (roughly two quarters stacked).

Red Onions

Remove outer skin before slicing. Use the mandolin to slice to ⅛" thickness (roughly two quarters stacked). Always wear a cutting glove and use the black feeder tool — no exceptions.
