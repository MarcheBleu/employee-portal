Tea Preparation

Iced Tea Batches — Black / Hibiscus / Bleu Tea

  1. Measure 90 g tea leaf and place into a reusable tea bag; tie off.
  2. Place bag in a large square tub with a lid.
  3. Pour 4 qt hot water and steep for 3 minutes.
  4. Add 8 qt cold water on top.
  5. Label with brew date/time and place in the walk-in cooler.
  6. Leave tea bag in for 16–24 hours, then remove.

Individual Tea Bags — Crescent Green / Rosella / Earl Grey / Ambrosia / Chamomile / Peppermint

  1. Weigh out 4–5 g tea leaves using a scale. (Peppermint: 3 g)
  2. Place measured leaves into an individual disposable tea bag.
  3. Tie off the tea bag.
  4. Place in the labeled canister for that tea variety.

Drink Prep

Cold Foam Base

  1. Add flavoring to a large squirt bottle: 4 pumps desired syrup (lavender, simple, etc.), 2 pumps desired sauce (caramel), or 2 oz puree (brambleberry or strawberry).
  2. Add ⅔ qt whipping cream to the bottle.
  3. Add food coloring as needed: salted caramel gets a hint of orange, strawberry gets a hint of red, brambleberry gets a hint of red and purple.
  4. Top off the squeeze bottle and shake until fully mixed.
  5. Label with food prep label and place in milk fridge.

Strawberry Puree

  1. Cut the tops off 3 clamshells of washed strawberries and cut in half.
  2. Place in the blender base.
  3. Add 1 cup granulated sugar and ¼ cup strawberry gelatin base.
  4. Blend on setting 1, then pulse 5 times.
